# How to Build a Beach Club
## Scaling and Building an International Brand

**Author:** Peter Higney  
**Started:** February 25, 2026  
**Stage:** Brain Dump / Collection

## Concept
The definitive book on building a beach club brand and scaling it internationally. Memoir + playbook format. No book like this exists.

## Comparable Books
- Danny Meyer — *Setting the Table* (restaurants)
- Bill Marriott — *Without Reservations* (hotels)  
- Will Guidara — *Unreasonable Hospitality* (fine dining)
- Micah Solomon — *The Heart of Hospitality*

## Process
1. **Brain Dump** — Peter sends voice notes, I transcribe and tag by theme
2. **Outline** — Build chapter structure from raw material
3. **Draft** — Chapter by chapter, in Peter's voice
4. **Edit & Polish**
