1. NEW HOTEL/RESORT CONSTRUCTION — SOUTH FLORIDA

Grand Hyatt Miami Beach

  • Location: Miami Beach (adjacent to Convention Center)
  • Developer/Owner: Terra Group / convention center partnership
  • Timeline: Groundbreaking May 2025 → Opening 2027
  • Scope: $600M, 800 rooms (52 suites), 4 floors meeting space, 5 F&B venues, resort pool deck, pedestrian bridge to convention center
  • Source: miamishome.com, meetingsmags.com

Waldorf Astoria Hotel & Residences Miami

  • Location: 300 Biscayne Blvd, Downtown Miami
  • Developer/Owner: PMG / Greybrook / managed by Hilton
  • Timeline: Topped 50th floor Dec 2025 → Opening 2028
  • Scope: 100-story supertall tower, 205 hotel rooms, 387 residences
  • Source: floridayimby.com

Miami Design District Hotel & Residences (David Chipperfield)

  • Location: 3995 N Miami Ave, Design District
  • Developer/Owner: Raycliff Capital / Fort Partners / Dacra / L Catterton / Brookfield / Constellation Hotels
  • Timeline: UDRB approved Jan 2026 → Pre-construction sales launching
  • Scope: 26-story hotel tower (85 rooms + 20 condo-hotel units), 12-story condo tower (143 units), 55K SF retail. David Chipperfield architect
  • Source: commercialobserver.com, floridayimby.com

The Sunny (Sunny Isles Beach)

  • Location: Sunny Isles Beach (former Newport Beachside)
  • Developer/Owner: HB Capital Group
  • Timeline: Opening Feb 2026
  • Scope: 339-room oceanfront transformation, private beach, spa, gym, 6 F&B outlets
  • Source: miaminewtimes.com

Hyatt Centric Wynwood

  • Location: Wynwood, Miami
  • Timeline: Opening 2026–2027
  • Source: miamiandbeaches.com

AC Hotel Miami Gardens City Center

  • Location: Miami Gardens
  • Timeline: Opening 2026–2027
  • Source: miamiandbeaches.com

2. MAJOR HOTEL RENOVATIONS & REMODELS — SOUTH FLORIDA

Delano Miami Beach

  • Location: South Beach
  • Owner/Operator: Ennismore
  • Timeline: Reopening March 2026
  • Scope: Comprehensive reimagining of iconic 1947 Art Deco property. Redesigned rooms, multiple new dining/bar concepts, wellness studio, revival of Rose Bar. Preserved terrazzo floors, white columns, famous pool
  • Source: miaminewtimes.com, hoteldive.com

Diplomat Beach Resort → Signia by Hilton Hollywood

  • Location: Hollywood, FL
  • Owner/Operator: Hilton (rebrand)
  • Timeline: Spring 2026
  • Scope: Full renovation — refreshed guest rooms, updated public spaces, new infinity pool, rebranded Palmea Kitchen restaurant
  • Source: miaminewtimes.com

Loews Miami Beach Hotel

  • Location: Miami Beach
  • Timeline: Completed winter 2025/26
  • Scope: $55M renovation of 790 rooms/suites, two new restaurants (Bistro Collins, The Sushi Bar)
  • Source: miamilivingmagazine.com

Royal Palm South Beach (Marriott Tribute Portfolio)

  • Location: South Beach
  • Timeline: 2026 (date TBD)
  • Scope: Full reimagining — redesigned rooms with Miami modernism/French Riviera aesthetic, revitalized pool and bar areas
  • Source: miaminewtimes.com, miamiandbeaches.com

The Beach Club at Boca Raton

  • Location: Boca Raton
  • Owner: The Boca Raton (MSD Partners)
  • Timeline: Completed late 2024/2025
  • Scope: $120–130M renovation. 210 refreshed rooms/suites (tripled suite inventory), new restaurants, cocktail lounge, oceanfront café, enhanced fitness, oceanside event spaces
  • Source: luxurytraveladvisor.com, resident.com

The Seagate (Delray Beach)

  • Timeline: Phase 1 complete
